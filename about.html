My Journey

Welcome!

Hello, I’m Kai, a 19-year-old artist and aspiring web designer!

I first started drawing when I was 10, and it’s something that has brought me joy through some of my most stressful moments. Some of my biggest inspirations for my art have been Pokemon, nature and animals. I love bright colors and eye-catching designs and I hope that anyone who looks at my art will end up leaving with a smile on their face.
